ABES Engineering College, Ghaziabad

ABES Engineering College is a renowned engineering institution located in Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh, India. It offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various engineering disciplines. The college is known for its quality education, experienced faculty, and state-of-the-art infrastructure.

  • Computer Science and Engineering
  • Electronics and Communication Engineering
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Civil Engineering
  • Information Technology
  • Electrical Engineering
